About Echo

Echo, the official magazine of the Students Union of CEV, is a harmonious blend of creativity and vision. It captures the essence of student expression through stories, poems, and reflections, giving voice to the challenges of today and the dreams of tomorrow. Crafted with passion, Echo is a testament to the vibrant spirit of our campus.
